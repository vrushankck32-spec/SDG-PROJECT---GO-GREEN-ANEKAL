Why Anekal

Anekal represents strong agricultural potential combined with limited access to structured livelihood opportunities for women.

Local Implementation

Community-Led Monitoring & Transparency

Go Green Anekal trains local women to monitor floriculture plots using QR-enabled systems. This enables real-time tracking, transparent reporting, and direct community participation in CSR-funded sustainability initiatives.
