About the Initiative

Designing CSR for Real Impact

Overview

Go Green Anekal addresses rural livelihood inequality and ineffective CSR impact measurement through a scalable, community-driven sustainability model.

The Challenge

Where Traditional CSR Falls Short

In rural regions, women lack access to stable livelihood opportunities, agricultural land remains underutilized, and CSR initiatives often operate without transparent, real-time impact tracking at the grassroots level.

Our Approach

A Community-Integrated CSR Ecosystem

Go Green Anekal connects corporate CSR investments directly with rural floriculture initiatives through technology-enabled monitoring and women-led field operations.
